// About the Faculty

Bridging Academia & Innovation

"Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire."

I'm Mr. Suraj Bhoite, an Assistant Professor in the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Dr. D. Y. Patil College of Engineering & Innovation, with over 7 years of experience in teaching, research, and academic development. My work is dedicated to advancing Computer Science education and fostering innovation in emerging technologies.

My research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, and Educational Technology. I have published more than 9 research papers in reputed international journals and conferences, I hold 4 patents for innovative technological solutions, and I have secured 6 copyrights for original intellectual contributions.

I teach courses in Data Science, Data Structures, Theory of Computation, and Machine Learning, and I am deeply passionate about mentoring students and cultivating an environment that encourages curiosity, critical thinking, and continuous learning.
